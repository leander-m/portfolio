Intro

A visual language and UI overhaul for data-heavy cybersecurity products, focused on making complex information easier to scan, compare and act on. The work brought more clarity, consistency and polish to dashboards, reporting and platform experiences through the Atlas look and feel.

Cybersecurity products are dense by nature. Users need to understand risk, spot patterns, compare changes over time and move quickly from insight to action. Across dashboards and reporting experiences, the challenge was not only to display more data, but to make that data easier to understand without overwhelming the interface.

This work focused on refining the visual language for complex, data-heavy product surfaces. That included improving hierarchy, spacing, contrast, chart presentation, layout structure and the way supporting information was surfaced around key metrics. The aim was to create interfaces that felt clearer, calmer and more deliberate, while still supporting the level of detail expected in enterprise cybersecurity workflows.

A major part of the work was developing the Atlas look and feel: a more polished visual direction for the platform that could bring consistency across dashboards, reports and wider product experiences. Atlas helped define how the product should use depth, colour, typography, density and visual emphasis to make complex workflows feel more modern and easier to read.

The overhaul also looked beyond individual screens. Data visualisation, reporting, cards, tables and dashboard patterns needed to feel like part of the same system. By aligning these patterns around shared visual principles, the platform could present information more consistently and help teams design from a common foundation.

The result was a clearer and more scalable approach to data-heavy UI. Instead of every dashboard or report solving hierarchy in a different way, the platform had a stronger visual foundation for presenting complex information with confidence, consistency and craft.
