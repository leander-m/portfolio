Intro

A unified design system built to support multiple product themes across Rapid7's security platform, bridging the gap between design and engineering.

The project began with an audit of existing component libraries and brand guidelines across the product suite. From there, we established a token-based architecture in Figma that could generate production-ready code, enabling seamless handoff and reducing implementation drift.
