He works across reusable patterns, scalable components, data visualisation, platform consistency and design tooling. His practice sits between product design and implementation, with an emphasis on clear hierarchy, polished UI and systems that help teams design and build more consistently.

His recent work has focused on enterprise cybersecurity products and design systems.
